\item[\textsc{L5.\ Kinetic-regime chemistry only.}] All Phase-1 simulations
produce centre-of-wafer [F] in the few-$\times 10^{20}$~m$^{-3}$ range, safely
below Mettler's $\sim 1.1 \times 10^{21}$~m$^{-3}$ diffusion-limited threshold
(his Fig.~4.9 and Table~4.4)~\cite{Mettler2025}, so the present [F] predictions
compare cleanly to his kinetic-regime probe data.  Extending to higher
densities (e.g.\ higher power, or NF$_3$ chemistry) would require coupling a
near-surface reactant-product transport equation (WF$_6$ or SiF$_4$) to
resolve the depletion layer.
